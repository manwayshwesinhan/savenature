At Saving Nature

Our mission is to rescue endangered species from extinction and communities from environmental destruction.

SAVING BIODIVERSITY

The world is in the midst of a mass extinction crisis, losing species at 1,000 times the natural background rate. As a result of human population growth and ever-increasing consumption, threatened and endangered species are facing habitat loss, introduction of exotic species, and climate disruption.

FIGHTING CLIMATE CHANGE

Our unrelenting carbon dioxide emissions have surpassed the planet’s ability to absorb these greenhouse gases. With this imbalance, we now face climate disruption and species extinctions. Even under the most optimistic scenarios, we aren’t going to reduce the high levels of carbon dioxide anytime soon.

PLANTING TREES

There is still time to save threatened and endangered species and vulnerable communities. We can recover and restore some of what has been lost and thoughtfully balance human activity with conservation actions to preserve our future. We plant the right trees in the right places to safeguard biodiversity, protect nature, and fight climate change.
